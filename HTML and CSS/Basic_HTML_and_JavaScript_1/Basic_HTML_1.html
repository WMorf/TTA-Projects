Goblinoids

Goblins

Goblins are a race of small and numerous goblinoids, often living in underground caverns near the surface known as lairs.

Hoboblins

Hobgoblins are larger, stronger, smarter, and more menacing forms of goblinoids than goblins

BugBears

Bugbears are a massive humanoid race distantly related to, but larger and stronger than, goblins and hobgoblins
